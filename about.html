The Beginning

Why We Built This

It started with a frustration we both shared. As developers who had been through campus placements and internship applications ourselves, we kept seeing the same thing — incredibly talented students getting overlooked because they had no portfolio, or a badly thrown-together template.

Recruiters spend an average of 6 seconds on a resume. A portfolio website changes that entirely. It gives them something to explore — your personality, your projects, your actual ability — not just bullet points on paper.

So in early 2025, Sujay and Ashish built PortfolioForge — removing every barrier between a student and a professional digital presence. Fast delivery. Real code. Honest pricing. No templates, no builders, no shortcuts. Everything hand-coded from scratch, specifically for each client.
