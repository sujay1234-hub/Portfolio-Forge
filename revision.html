FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about revisions? We've got answers.

What counts as "one revision"? ›
One revision = one category of update (e.g., adding one certificate OR adding one internship). If you need to add 3 different things across different sections, you'd submit 3 requests. Bundled items in the same category (e.g., adding 3 certificates at once) count as one revision.
How do I pay the ₹15? ›
After you submit the form, we'll send you payment details on WhatsApp. You can pay via GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, or any UPI app. Just send a screenshot of the payment and we'll start immediately.
How long does a revision take? ›
Standard revisions are completed within 24 hours (usually 6–12 hours). Express delivery (within 4–6 hours) is available for an additional ₹10.
Do I need to have bought my portfolio from you? ›
Yes, the revision service is currently available only for portfolios built by PortfolioForge. We need access to your original source files, which we retain from the initial build.
Can you rewrite my bio from scratch? ›
Absolutely! Share your target role, key skills, and a couple of lines about yourself, and we'll craft a professional, impactful About section that stands out to recruiters.
What if I'm not happy with the update? ›
We offer one free minor correction round if something doesn't look right. We aim to get it perfect the first time, but if you want a small tweak (wording, spacing, position), just let us know within 48 hours.
Can I request multiple changes for ₹15? ›
₹15 covers one category of update. If you have many updates across multiple sections, we recommend our bundled revision pack (3 revisions for ₹40) — just mention it on WhatsApp and we'll set it up for you.
